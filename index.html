Asimo Robot

Development on the Honda ASIMO robot has stopped, according to multiple reports. Honda says it will continue researching humanoid robots,
but they won’t have the ASIMO name. Honda’s main focus will shift to using some of ASIMO’s technology to develop robots for more practical applications, including nursing and road transport.
